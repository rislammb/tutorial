      <h1>HTML Heading</h1>
      <p>
        HTML has <b>6</b> type of heading, &lt;h1&gt; to &lt;h6&gt;. <br>
        <b>&lt;h1&gt;</b> is most important heading. <br>
        <b>&lt;h6&gt;</b> is least important heading. <br>
      </p>
      <h2>HTML Heading Example</h2>
      <ul class="example">
        <li>&lt;<span>!DOCTYPE html</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>html</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>head</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>title</span>&gt;Page Title&lt;<span>/title</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>/head</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>body</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>h1</span>&gt;Most important Heading&lt;<span>/h1</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>h2</span>&gt;Second important Heading&lt;<span>/h2</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>h6</span>&gt;Least Heading&lt;<span>/h6</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>/body</span>&gt;</li>
        <li>&lt;<span>/html</span>&gt;</li>
      </ul>
